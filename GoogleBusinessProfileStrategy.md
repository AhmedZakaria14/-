# Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization Strategy for Nashar Hub (KSA)

## Objective
Rank in the "Local Pack" (Top 3 map results) for keywords like "Digital Marketing Agency Riyadh", "Web Design Jeddah", and "SEO Company Saudi Arabia".

## 1. Profile Setup & Verification
*   **Business Name:** Use the exact legal name "Nashar Hub". Do *not* stuff keywords (e.g., "Nashar Hub - Best SEO Agency") as this risks suspension.
*   **Address:** Use a real physical address in Riyadh (e.g., Olaya District) or Jeddah. If you are a service-area business without a public office, hide the address but set the service area to "Riyadh", "Jeddah", "Dammam".
*   **Phone Number:** Use a local Saudi number (+966) if possible, or the primary business WhatsApp number.
*   **Website URL:** Link to the Saudi landing page (`https://nasharhub.com/saudi`) instead of the home page to increase relevance for KSA searches.

## 2. Category Selection
*   **Primary Category:** "Marketing Agency" or "Internet Marketing Service".
*   **Secondary Categories:**
    *   "Website Designer"
    *   "Advertising Agency"
    *   "E-commerce Service"
    *   "Software Company"

## 3. "From the Business" Description (SEO Optimized)
Write a 750-character description including key services and locations.
*   *Draft:* "Nashar Hub is a leading digital marketing agency in Riyadh, specializing in SEO, PPC (Snapchat & TikTok Ads), and E-commerce development (Salla & Zid). We help Saudi businesses grow online with data-driven strategies. Contact us for web design, social media management, and branding services."

## 4. Photos & Videos (Crucial for KSA)
Saudi users rely heavily on visuals.
*   **Cover Photo:** High-quality branded image.
*   **Team Photos:** Real photos of the team working (builds trust).
*   **Office/Work:** Photos of the workspace or meetings.
*   **Video:** A 30-second intro video in Arabic welcoming clients.

## 5. Reviews Strategy (The Trust Factor)
*   **Goal:** Get 10+ 5-star reviews in the first month.
*   **Action:** Send a direct review link to all past and current happy clients.
*   **Reply to Reviews:** Reply to *every* review (positive or negative) within 24 hours. Use keywords in your replies (e.g., "Thank you for trusting Nashar Hub with your **SEO project**...").

## 6. Posts & Updates
Treat GBP like a social media channel.
*   **Weekly Posts:** Share blog post summaries, new case studies, or offers.
*   **Offers:** "Free SEO Audit for Riyadh Businesses" (Link to the contact form).
*   **Events:** If hosting a webinar or workshop.

## 7. Q&A Section
*   **Seed Questions:** Ask and answer your own common questions.
    *   Q: "Do you work with Salla stores?"
    *   A: "Yes, we specialize in scaling Salla and Zid stores using paid ads and SEO."
    *   Q: "Where is your office located?"
    *   A: "We serve clients across Saudi Arabia, with our main operations focused on Riyadh and Jeddah."

## 8. Products & Services
*   List all services (SEO, PPC, Web Design) with prices (or "Free Quote") and descriptions.
*   Link each service to its specific page on the website.
