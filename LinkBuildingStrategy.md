# Off-Page SEO Strategy for Nashar Hub (Saudi Market 2025)

## Objective
Increase Domain Authority (DA) and drive referral traffic to rank #1 for "Digital Marketing Agency Saudi Arabia" and related Arabic keywords.

## 1. The "Link Magnet" Strategy (Implemented)
We have built a **Free Packages Wizard** (`/packages`). Interactive tools attract more backlinks than standard blog posts.
*   **Action:** Pitch this tool to Saudi business news sites (e.g., Argaam, Maaal) as a "Free tool to help Saudi SMEs budget for marketing."

## 2. Local Citations & Directories (NAP Consistency)
Ensure Name, Address, and Phone Number are identical across all listings.
*   **Google Business Profile:** (Priority #1) - Get verified in Riyadh.
*   **Saudi Business Directories:**
    *   Daleeli.com
    *   Saudi Yellow Pages
    *   Eye of Riyadh
    *   Zawya (Saudi section)
*   **Action:** Submit Nashar Hub to these 4 directories this week.

## 3. Digital PR & Guest Posting
Target high-authority Saudi and regional publications.
*   **Target Sites:**
    *   *Sabq* or *Okaz* (Paid PR often required, but high value).
    *   *Tech-wd.com* (Technology news).
    *   *Unlimit-tech.com* (Tech reviews).
*   **Content Angles:**
    *   "How Vision 2030 is changing Digital Marketing."
    *   "Why Saudi E-commerce stores fail in their first year."

## 4. Social Signals
While not direct ranking factors, they drive traffic which *is* a ranking factor.
*   **LinkedIn:** Publish case studies (like the "Perfume Brand" one in our blog) as PDF carousels.
*   **Twitter/X:** Create threads summarizing our blog posts (e.g., "5 SEO Mistakes in KSA").

## 5. Competitor Backlink Gap Analysis
*   **Competitors:** 
    *   X-Element
    *   Glance
    *   Ubrand
*   **Strategy:** Use Ahrefs/Semrush to see who links to them, and pitch Nashar Hub as a better resource.

## 6. Sponsorships
*   Sponsor local tech meetups in Riyadh (e.g., at The Garage or Code).
*   Get a backlink from the event page (usually high DA, .sa domain).
