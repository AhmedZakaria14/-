# Local SEO Plan: Riyadh Dominance 🇸🇦

To rank for **"شركة تسويق بالرياض" (Marketing Company in Riyadh)** and **"تصميم مواقع بالرياض" (Web Design in Riyadh)**, you need to send strong local signals to Google.

## 1. Google Business Profile (GBP) Optimization
*   **Name:** Use your brand name + a *single* main keyword if possible (e.g., "Nashar Hub - شركة تسويق الكتروني وتصميم مواقع"). *Warning: Don't keyword stuff too heavily or you risk suspension.*
*   **Categories:**
    *   Primary: `Marketing Agency` (وكالة تسويق)
    *   Secondary: `Website Designer` (مصمم مواقع ويب), `Internet Marketing Service` (خدمة التسويق عبر الإنترنت), `Advertising Agency` (وكالة إعلانية).
*   **Description:** Write a 750-character description including "Riyadh", "Saudi Arabia", and core services in Arabic.
*   **Service Area:** Define Riyadh and specific neighborhoods (e.g., Olaya, Malaz).
*   **Products/Services:** Add every service from your keyword list as a "Product" in GBP with a photo and description.

## 2. Local Landing Page Strategy (`/riyadh-marketing`)
Create a dedicated page for Riyadh that acts as a local funnel.

*   **URL:** `yourdomain.com/saudi-arabia/riyadh-digital-marketing`
*   **Embed Map:** Embed your Google Maps location in the footer or contact section.
*   **Local Content:**
    *   Mention local landmarks or business districts ("Serving clients in Olaya Business District").
    *   Use local phone number format (+966 11...).
    *   Showcase clients specifically from Riyadh.
*   **Schema Markup:** Use `LocalBusiness` JSON-LD specifically for this page, linking it to your GBP map URL.

## 3. Citations & NAP Consistency
*   Ensure your **Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP)** are exactly the same across all directories.
*   **Target Directories:**
    *   Google Maps
    *   Apple Maps
    *   Saudi Yellow Pages
    *   Local business directories (Daleeli, Haraj - if applicable for services).

## 4. Reviews Strategy (The Trust Factor)
*   **Keyword in Reviews:** Ask happy clients to mention the service they bought in their review.
    *   *Good:* "Excellent service."
    *   *Better:* "Nashar Hub is the **best marketing company in Riyadh**. They helped us with **Google Ads**."
*   **Reply to Reviews:** Reply to every review within 24 hours using keywords naturally (e.g., "Thank you for choosing us for your **web design** project.").
